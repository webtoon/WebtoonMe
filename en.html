Through the interactive webtoon <Met>, NAVER WEBTOON introduced a technology that naturally translates the selfie of a reader into webtoon style and inserting it into the webtoon itself. This webtoon, <Met>, received a positive response at the time, many applauding the element of participation in the story instead of simply reading a displayed work as a reader (Article in Korean).

The <WebtoonMe Project> is now taking another technical step forward from the conversion technology used in <Met> and aims for a more 'webtoon-like translation while maintaining my unique characteristics'. This article will introduce the technologies of the <WebtoonMe Project> while sharing the various and interesting results.

What are you most curious about when you think of a 'technology that converts my photos into a webtoon'? A lot of people could be wondering about how their face can be transformed into webtoon characters. And that is the same curiosity that we started when developing the deep learning-based 'face translation' technology for this project.

After several trials and errors, the deep learning-based face transformation technology started to show signs of completion. To deliver even more joy, the project was expanded to include even the background when transforming photos instead of simply focusing on the face. But we ran into an unexpected problem, unlike the previous face transformation. The various objects (e.g., face, body parts, background, etc.) in a photo all needed a different 'style' that we were unaware of, and the results seemed awkward without that organic consideration.

While analyzing the data, we came to a conclusion that objects in the webtoon dataset had a unique 'style' property unlike the natural photo dataset that is normally used in deep learning research. Then, we decided to learn about the webtoon creation process in-depth to understand how different works stylized different objects.

One of the biggest advantages of researching in the WEBTOON AI department is that we have the special opportunity to 'directly interview famous creators who are interested in AI technologies'. After interviewing several creators, we learned a few things: the 'foreground (including faces)' and 'backgrounds' are created using different tools, there are many points to consider in order to create an organic style, and that retouching is necessary in order to blend them inside the scene naturally.

And we used that insight in the following method. First, we built a webtoon dataset consisting of various pieces such as the face, body, objects, and nature, then created various deep-learning models to reduce the style difference between the foreground and the background. Then, we applied several post-processing algorithms imitating the retouching techniques of different creators to improve the quality of the results more naturally.

The conversion technology developed through the project can be used to increase the immersion of readers in webtoons like the work <Met> did in the past. We are also working on fine-tuning it as a creative tool to help the webtoon creation process. How can conversion technology be used to create webtoons?

As the industry leader, NAVER WEBTOON is making various efforts to expand the webtoon creation ecosystem. Beginning with the <WEBTOON AI PAINTER> earlier introduced, we are working on a technology to help people without professional drawing skills to create webtoons more easily. And "Auto Drawing" (Article in Korean) is also one of the technologies that we are working on focusing on as part of the project. In other words, we have high hopes that the technology that allows individuals to create unique webtoons using their photos of people and backgrounds and the conversion technology offering various creative inspirations will contribute to webtoon creation in the future.
